About this site
The Mission

What Is TorahMoment?

TorahMoment is a resource for Torah learning — a place to encounter ideas that illuminate Jewish life, deepen one's understanding of halacha and machshava, and connect with the timeless wisdom of Torah in a meaningful, accessible way.

The site hosts divrei Torah, audio and video shiurim, and written resources spanning a range of topics — from the weekly parasha and halacha l'maaseh to Jewish philosophy and the teachings of great gedolim.

The Person Behind It

About the Author

TorahMoment is the personal project of a Torah-committed Jew with a lifelong love of learning and teaching. Raised in Brooklyn with deep roots in Torah scholarship, the site reflects a commitment to bringing substantive, honest Torah content to a broader audience.

The divrei Torah shared here draw on years of learning under distinguished rebbeim, personal study, and a belief that every Jew deserves access to Torah ideas that are both rigorous and alive. The YouTube channel @torahmoment offers video shiurim that complement the written content on the site.

The goal is simple: to make each learning experience feel like a genuine moment — one that stays with you.
